About Plugin

Plugin (PLI), a Secure, Scalable Decentralized Oracle Platform, provides cost-effective solutions to any smart contract, which runs on XDC Network. Pugin enables smart contract to connect with the real-time world and the data that it receives from the data feed partners, is trustable by maintaining a high degree of security. Off-chain computation takes care of receiving a feed from multiple providers and aggregates the same.

Please click on goplugin.co for more info.
